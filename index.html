Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2026

Raj Attendance ("we", "our", or "us") operates an institute management mobile application designed to manage student attendance, fees, notes, and communication ("Service"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app.

1. Information We Collect

a) Personal Information

  • Name
  • Email address
  • User role (Admin, Teacher, Student)
  • Login credentials (secured via Firebase Authentication)

b) Student & Academic Data

  • Attendance records
  • Fees/payment details
  • Notes and academic content

c) Device & Usage Data

  • Device type and OS version
  • App usage activity
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

d) Location Data

Location may be collected (if enabled) for attendance verification and related features.

e) Media & Storage Access

  • Camera (to capture images if required)
  • Storage (to upload/download notes and files)
---

2. How We Use Information

  • To manage student attendance and records
  • To track and display fee details
  • To provide access to notes and study materials
  • To authenticate users securely
  • To send important notifications and updates
  • To improve app performance and user experience
---

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google) – for authentication and database services
  • OneSignal – for push notifications

These services may collect and process data according to their respective privacy policies.

---

4. Push Notifications

We use OneSignal to send notifications such as attendance updates, fee reminders, and announcements. Users can disable notifications anytime via device settings.

---

5. Data Storage & Security

We take appropriate security measures to protect your data. Data is stored securely using Firebase services. However, no system is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

---

6. Data Sharing

We do NOT sell or rent user data. Data is only used for app functionality and may be shared with trusted services (Firebase, OneSignal) as required.

---

7. User Rights

  • Access your data
  • Request correction of incorrect data
  • Request deletion of your account/data
  • Opt-out of notifications

8. Children’s Privacy

This app may be used by students under institutional supervision. We do not knowingly collect personal data directly from children without proper authorization.
